About Us


Our vision is for more people to be more active, more often. Fitness Hub is a not-for-profit industry association, that exists to professionalise the fitness industry through engaging in partnerships, advocacy, delivering education, quality and accreditation. Fitness Hub supports the industry to deliver an environment for more people to adopt a healthy, active lifestyle. Key Strategic Goals This vision is to be delivered by: Proactive Leadership – Fitness Hub is the credible, recognised voice of the industry and is influential in driving improvement, innovation and reform. Meaningful Engagement – Fitness Hub provides value to stakeholders, resulting in strong, mutually beneficial relationships. Service Excellence – Fitness Hub embodies and enables ongoing quality improvement, building industry credibility and driving growth Financial and Operational Sustainability – Fitness Hub creates an environment in which Fitness Hub and its members drive sustainable and efficient operations
